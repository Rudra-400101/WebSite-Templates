About the Program

Techpile's Winter Training program is a specialized initiative designed to provide students with the opportunity to enhance their skills and knowledge during the winter break. This intensive training program offers hands-on experience and practical learning to prepare participants for the challenges of the modern workforce.

Program Tenure & Timing

This Program commence Every year at Techpile in the month of November/December and Ends after nearly 4 weeks. The program duration and Tenure varies every academic year depending on Students College Examinations, Batch Availability, Duration of Time Alloted to Sudents and on Multiple other Factors. For more Information on Current Academic Year's Batch & Timings, Contact us on the given mobile number or enquiry form.

How to Join?

Joining our Winter Training program is straightforward. Interested candidates can apply online through our website or visit our campus for registration and enrollment assistance.

Who Can Join?

Students pursuing their Diploma, Undergraduate or Postgraduate Degrees in Computer Science/Information Technology or related fields are eligible to apply for our winter Training program.

This Training is most favorable for
  • Diploma in CS/IT/EX/EE/CE/ME
  • B.Tech/B.E in CS/IT/EC/EE/CE/ME
  • P.G Diploma in Computer Applications (P.G.D.C.A.)
  • Bachelors in Computer Applications(B.C.A)
  • Masters in Computer Applications(M.C.A)

Is prior programming experience required to join the Winter Training program?

No, our program caters to students of all levels and is Built to prepare Trainees from beginners to advanced.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion of the program?

Yes, participants who successfully complete the program will receive an Enrolled Physical Certificate from Techpile.

Can this training be held online?

Yes, We started online training's during the pandemic to help Students with remote learning. However, we highly recommend offline training for proper hands-on learning and interactive sessions.

Will I have access to course materials and resources after completing the winter Training program?

Yes, participants will have access to course materials, resources, and recordings for future reference even after completing the program. We believe in providing continued support to our alumni to aid in their ongoing learning and development.

Can I expect any networking opportunities or industry exposure during the program?

Absolutely! Techpile organizes guest lectures, workshops, and networking events to provide participants with exposure to industry professionals, experts, and potential employers. These interactions offer valuable insights and networking opportunities for career advancement.
